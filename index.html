FAQ

+ - What services do you offer?

In short, we offer Web and Mobile Development, Design and Front End Development. Clients can use any individual service, all services at once or any combination thereof.

+ - What technologies do you use?

On the back end, we are a Ruby on Rails shop. While we are skilled at other languages, our standards and quality controls are all built around RoR.

On the front end, we are experts at HTML5, CSS3, Sass, Haml, Javascript, Coffeescript, AJAX and many more.

On the mobile side, we are skilled in Objective C for iOS and Java for Android. We’re also experienced with mobile frameworks such as Rhodes, jQuery Mobile and others.

+ - How is all planned?

We use an altered Scrum Agile Development process that we’ve optimized for our team. In short, we run 1 week sprints and iterate as fast as humanly possible. We do daily “standup” calls and weekly planning meetings. At the end of each week we hope to have built a new feature, launch something publicly or accomplished another goal.

+ - How do you host my app?

We highly recommend hosting your web application in the cloud. We’re partners of Heroku and Engineyard, but we can go directly on AWS or use other hosting platforms.

+ - Source Control?

We are in with Github due to the number of tools integrated and many features for collaborative development. But if you work on a different source control, we are experts on Mercurial and SVN too.
